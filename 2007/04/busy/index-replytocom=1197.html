In the meantime, here is a sneak peak of some of the things I’ve been working on!

    The picnic sandwich looks absolutely scrumptous. I do something similiar: I get a round, rustic loaf. Hollow it out. Stuff it with whatever veg is around (tomatoes, lettuce, arugula, etc.). Add pitted olives and capers. Pour on extra virgin olive oil. Maybe add feta or someother cheese if I’m looking for something extra hardy. And wrap and put something heavy on top (pot) to compress for a few hours. By the time we eat, flavor is infused throughout and it is amazing.
